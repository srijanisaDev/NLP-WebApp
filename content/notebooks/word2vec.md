## Word Embedding

In natural language processing , word embedding is used for the representation of words for text analysis , typically in the form of real-valued vector that encodes the meaning of the word such that the words that are closer in the vector space are expected to be similar in meaning.

## What is Word2vec

-> It is a word embedding techinique which converts word to vectors/ numericals because the machine cannot process the words directly .
-> It is able to preserve the semantic meaning of a word i.e it can conclude that joy and happy have same meaning.
-> It was able to reduce the dimentionality more than the BOW/Tfidf huge dimensions whereas applying Word2vec the dimentions ranges from (100 - 300).
-> Computation is fast.
-> Dense vectors ....which solves the problem of overfitting.


## To increase the overall quality of word2vec 
1. Increase the training set
2. Increase the dimension of vectors
3. Increase window size
